Jay Fai Street Food

327 Maha Chai Road, Samran Rat, Phra Nakhon, Bangkok, 10200, Thailand.

Best time to visit: Morning to late afternoon to avoid long lines.

Specialties: Famous for crab omelets and Thai seafood dishes.

Banthat Thong Road

Located in the Siam area, reachable by BTS Skytrain (National Stadium Station) or MRT Subway (Hua Lamphong Station).

Best time to visit: Evening around 8 pm for full street food experience.

Specialties: Vibrant street food scene with noodles, satay, and desserts.

Or Tor Kor Market

Kamphaeng Phet Rd, Chatuchak, Bangkok, Thailand.

Best time to visit: Morning to early afternoon for fresh produce and street snacks.

Specialties: Fruits, local snacks, and authentic Thai ingredients.
